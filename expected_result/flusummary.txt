Free Flu Shots While Supplies Last
Foothill College offers the seasonal flu vaccine to currently enrolled Foothill students at no charge while supplies last. To receive the vaccine, you must present photo identification and proof of current, valid enrollment in a Foothill course; there are no exceptions.&nbsp;The vaccine is available for $10 to Foothill College faculty,&nbsp;staff and retirees with valid ID. Payment by credit card or check is preferred.&nbsp;
